Donor Management System

Guides non-profit on the right course of action to inspire commitment to a cause, cultivate relationships with funders, and gives you the tools to perform these actions efficiently.

Core Features

Develop The Best Prospects

Each funder's philanthropic goals, decision-making cycle and interaction with the non-profit will be mapped out. Publicly available information will be silently monitored for the funder's reported wealth and philanthropy activity. Cumulatively, this takes the guesswork out of finding the funder's giving prospects and assists the non-profit to reach out at an opportune time.

The non-profit will be able to gauge if the funder's philanthropy focus is a good match with the fundee's activities, thereby unlocking more giving.

Build Good Relationships

Calling new funders to say 'Thank You' can increase funders retention rates. The system will prioritize the top 10 first-time funders you should call directly. You can also assign calls to your colleagues and onboard funders with a 'view-only' access to the system's dashboard for a high-level overview of your nonprofit's work.

Know How Funders Feel about You

2-way interactions with regular funders will significantly increase retention. The donor management system enables users to surveys to funders and measure satisfaction, commitment and trust towards the non-profit, without additional software.

The donor management software also integrates with WhatsApp Business messaging for the non-profit to build on organic conversations with funders.

A Digital Assistant In Your Pocket

Saw a funder in the news? Update the funder's profile conveniently, add a note on what you found, send a congratulatory message or assign a colleague to follow-up. Meeting with a funder? Look through last-minute research to remind you of their personal details.

Built-in speech recognition software to dictate the notes as the meeting goes, so you can focus on the conversation. After the meeting, capture the info while it is fresh in your mind.
